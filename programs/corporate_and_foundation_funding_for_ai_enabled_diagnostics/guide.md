## 1. Executive Summary

The "Innovations in Cost-Disruptive Tools for Diagnosis and Screening" Grand Challenge, funded by the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, seeks transformative, high-risk, high-reward innovations to address the significant barrier of limited access to affordable diagnosis in low- and middle-income countries (LMICs). The program aims to source cost-disruptive devices and $1-class diagnostic tests that materially reset the cost curve, enabling scalable screening, same-visit decision-making, and reconfigurable use cases. Proposals are particularly encouraged for novel sensing modalities, software-defined diagnostics, and AI-enabled or software-only approaches that fundamentally rethink how diagnosis or screening is performed. The initiative supports a staged portfolio from early concepts to later-stage adaptation and scale, with milestone-based awards ranging from approximately US$300,000 to US$1,000,000. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[3]](https://www.grandchallenges.org/about#:~:text=in%20Conflict%3A%20A%20Humanitarian%20Grand%20Challenge%20seeks%20life-saving%20or%20life-improving%20innovations%20to%20help%20the%20most%20vulnerable%20)

## 2. Program Overview

This Grand Challenge focuses on developing and deploying cost-disruptive diagnostic and screening tools for LMICs. Nearly half of the global population lacks essential diagnostic tests, with access almost nonexistent for up to 81% in the poorest settings, leading to delayed or missed disease detection. The program emphasizes solutions that align with the World Health Organization’s (WHO) ASSURED principles, promoting decentralization, low complexity, and affordability. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[3]](https://www.grandchallenges.org/about#:~:text=in%20Conflict%3A%20A%20Humanitarian%20Grand%20Challenge%20seeks%20life-saving%20or%20life-improving%20innovations%20to%20help%20the%20most%20vulnerable%20)

**Key Objectives:**
*   **Cost-Disruptive Tools:** Develop devices that amortize capital to near-zero incremental cost and consumable $1-class tests that significantly reduce costs in LMICs while meeting real-world deployment constraints. For screening, cost targets are per person screened; for diagnostic/monitoring, per test performed. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[4]](https://www.grandchallenges.org/grant-opportunities#:~:text=r%20Grand%20Challenges%20and%20related%20grant%20opportunities.%20Breakthrough%20Innovations%20to%20Significantly%20Reduce%20the%20Cost%20of%20Severe%20)
*   **Transformative Innovation:** Prioritize novel sensing modalities, software-defined diagnostics, and AI-enabled or software-only approaches that fundamentally change performance, cost structure, or deployment models. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[2]](https://wellcome.org/research-funding/schemes#:~:text=et%20Explorer%2011%20will%20go%20out%20of%20support%20and%20be%20retired%20on%20June%2015%2C%202022.%20For%20more%20information%20on%20upgrading%20please%20see%20brow)
*   **Scalable Solutions:** Advance cross-sector, platform, and multimodal solutions to enable scalable screening, same-visit decision-making, or reconfigurable use cases.
*   **Staged Portfolio:** Build a portfolio spanning high-risk early concepts to later-stage adaptation and scale, utilizing milestone-based awards aligned to clear technology readiness level (TRL) criteria. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[4]](https://www.grandchallenges.org/grant-opportunities#:~:text=r%20Grand%20Challenges%20and%20related%20grant%20opportunities.%20Breakthrough%20Innovations%20to%20Significantly%20Reduce%20the%20Cost%20of%20Severe%20)

**Cross-Cutting Design Criteria (Expectations):**
*   **Rapid results:** Provides actionable results during a single patient encounter.
*   **Ease of use:** Operable by minimally trained users in decentralized settings.
*   **LMIC robustness:** Functions reliably in low-resource environments (heat, dust, intermittent power, limited infrastructure).
*   **Cold-chain independence:** Minimizes or eliminates cold-chain requirements through thermostable reagents and temperature-stable device design.
*   **Consumables minimization:** Reduces reliance on disposable consumables and favors durable, reusable hardware architectures with negligible incremental cost per screening.
*   **Cost:** Demonstrates a credible pathway to approximately US$1 per test or near-zero incremental cost per person screened for devices.
*   **Transformative innovation:** Incorporates novel architectures such as multimodal sensing, high-order multiplexing, software-defined diagnostics, AI-enabled interpretation, or software-only approaches. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail)

**Additional Desirable Attributes:**
*   **Multi-disease capability:** Enables testing for multiple diseases from a single platform or workflow.
*   **Data traceability and surveillance integration:** Ensures diagnostic results are digitally structured, traceable, and interoperable with national and global surveillance systems.
*   **Modularity:** Supports expansion to additional analytes or conditions without major redesign.
*   **Environmental sustainability:** Minimizes environmental impact through biodegradable or recyclable consumables and responsible end-of-life disposal strategies.

**In-Scope Topic Areas:**
*   Tuberculosis (TB)
*   Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV)
*   Malaria
*   Sexually Transmitted Infections (STI)
*   Maternal and Newborn Health
*   Anemia and Women's Health
*   Nutrition Surveillance and Fortified-Food Monitoring
*   Enteric Diseases
*   Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs)
*   Emerging Pathogens and Syndromic Testing
*   Manufacturing Innovations

Cross-sector or cross-disease innovations are explicitly encouraged. Platforms or technologies initially developed for non-health, non-diagnostic, or different disease applications are eligible, provided a clear, technically credible, milestone-based plan to adapt the technology to at least one relevant use case is included.

## 5. Award Size & Budget

The Challenge offers multiple award sizes commensurate with technology maturity:

*   **Option A (Smaller Proof-of-Concept Awards):**
    *   Up to five awards of approximately **US$300,000** each.
    *   Grant term of up to **24 months**.
    *   Supports early feasibility and prototyping, including technically risky, out-of-the-box concepts.
*   **Option B (Mid-Level Awards):**
    *   Up to three awards of approximately **US$500,000** each.
    *   Grant term of up to **24 months**.
    *   Supports product refinement and early validation.
*   **Option C (Larger Awards):**
    *   Up to two awards of approximately **US$1,000,000** each.
    *   Grant term of up to **36 months**.
    *   Supports mature platforms or advanced adaptation toward verification and field readiness, with commensurate evidence of technical readiness, feasibility, and a clear pathway to validation.

Final award amounts, the number of awards at each level, and duration will depend on proposal quality and strategic fit. Applicants should request funding aligned with the scope and maturity of their proposed work and include a clear milestone plan proportionate to the support requested. Indirect costs will be considered and should be included in the budget for up to the grant amount awarded (subject to the Gates Foundation's indirect cost policy). [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[5]](https://www.gatesfoundation.org/about/how-we-work/grant-opportunities#:~:text=opportunities%20Grant%20applicant%20FAQ%20Strategic%20Investments%20Grant%20opportunities%20The%20foundation%20awards%20the%20majority%20of%20its%20gr)

*   **Emphasize Cost-Disruption:** Demonstrate a credible pathway to achieving approximately US$1 per test or near-zero incremental cost per person screened. Highlight how your innovation materially resets the cost curve. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[4]](https://www.grandchallenges.org/grant-opportunities#:~:text=r%20Grand%20Challenges%20and%20related%20grant%20opportunities.%20Breakthrough%20Innovations%20to%20Significantly%20Reduce%20the%20Cost%20of%20Severe%20)
*   **Propose Transformative Innovation:** Prioritize novel sensing modalities, software-defined diagnostics, AI-enabled or software-only approaches that fundamentally rethink existing methods. Avoid incremental improvements to well-established approaches unless they offer a significant step-change in cost or scalability. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[2]](https://wellcome.org/research-funding/schemes#:~:text=et%20Explorer%2011%20will%20go%20out%20of%20support%20and%20be%20retired%20on%20June%2015%2C%202022.%20For%20more%20information%20on%20upgrading%20please%20see%20brow)
*   **Ensure Operational Feasibility:** Detail how your tool will be rapid, easy to use by minimally trained users, robust in low-resource environments, cold-chain independent, and minimize consumables. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[3]](https://www.grandchallenges.org/about#:~:text=in%20Conflict%3A%20A%20Humanitarian%20Grand%20Challenge%20seeks%20life-saving%20or%20life-improving%20innovations%20to%20help%20the%20most%20vulnerable%20)
*   **Develop a Strong Workplan:** Provide a feasible workplan with clear milestones that are appropriate for the technology's maturity level. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[4]](https://www.grandchallenges.org/grant-opportunities#:~:text=r%20Grand%20Challenges%20and%20related%20grant%20opportunities.%20Breakthrough%20Innovations%20to%20Significantly%20Reduce%20the%20Cost%20of%20Severe%20)
*   **Justify Funding:** Ensure your requested funding level is well-supported by the technical readiness and feasibility evidence presented. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[5]](https://www.gatesfoundation.org/about/how-we-work/grant-opportunities#:~:text=opportunities%20Grant%20applicant%20FAQ%20Strategic%20Investments%20Grant%20opportunities%20The%20foundation%20awards%20the%20majority%20of%20its%20gr)
*   **Consider Platform Solutions:** If proposing a platform, explain how it can support multiple use cases to improve economics and sustainability. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[4]](https://www.grandchallenges.org/grant-opportunities#:~:text=r%20Grand%20Challenges%20and%20related%20grant%20opportunities.%20Breakthrough%20Innovations%20to%20Significantly%20Reduce%20the%20Cost%20of%20Severe%20)
*   **Embrace Openness:** Be prepared to commit to independent evaluation participation and comply with the Gates Foundation's open access policy, including sharing prototypes, reagents, and data for third-party assessment. [[1]](https://gcgh.grandchallenges.org/challenge/innovations-cost-disruptive-tools-diagnosis-and-screening#:~:text=ebinar%20on%20March%2030%2C%20from%208%3A30-9%3A30%20AM%20Pacific%20Time.%20This%20session%20will%20provide%20a%20comprehensive%20overview%20of%20the%20RFP%20detail) [[5]](https://www.gatesfoundation.org/about/how-we-work/grant-opportunities#:~:text=opportunities%20Grant%20applicant%20FAQ%20Strategic%20Investments%20Grant%20opportunities%20The%20foundation%20awards%20the%20majority%20of%20its%20gr)
*   **Leverage Desirable Attributes:** Incorporate multi-disease capability, data traceability for surveillance, modularity, and environmental sustainability where possible.
*   **Attend the Webinar:** Register and submit questions in advance for the dedicated webinar on March 30, 2026, for a comprehensive overview and Q&A. If unable to attend live, the recording will be available.
